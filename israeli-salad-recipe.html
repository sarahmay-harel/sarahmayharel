Israeli Salad!!!

Ingredients

  • 1 english cucumber
  • 1 large carrot
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1 jalapeno
  • 1 red or green bell pepper
  • 1 shallot or small onion (optional)
  • 1 cup kalamata olives
  • 1/2 cup fresh minced parsley (optional)
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Steps

  1. Dice the cucumber, carrot, tomatoes, jalapeno, pepper, and shallot.
  2. Cut olives into halves or quarters.
  3. Toss together with juice of lemon, parsley, and olive oil.
  4. The acids from the lemon and tomato will marinate the other ingredients with time, so best when prepared a day or two before eating.
